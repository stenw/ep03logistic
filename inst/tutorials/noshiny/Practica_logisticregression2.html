In this exercise we analyze the fracture data with logistic regression in R. This is a study concerning neurological deficit in thoracolumbar burst fractures. Between January 1981 and January 1991, 139 patients with thora-columbar or lumbar burst fractures were treated in the Department of Orthopaedics and the Department of Neurosurgery of the Academic Hospital Dijkzigt of the Erasmus University of Rotterdam (Fontijne WPJ et al., J.of Bone & Joint Surgery, in press). A.o., the following variables were observed:

  • ID: Subject number

  • DEF: the occurrence of neurological deficit (‘yes’ or ‘no’)

  • LOC: the localization of the fracture (‘lumbar’, ‘thoracolumbar’ or ‘thoracic’)

  • STEN: percentage spinal canal stenosis
